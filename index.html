How To Play Sudoku

  • Pick a Puzzle – Choose your favorite level and get cozy
  • Fill the Grid – Each row, column, and box needs numbers 1–9, no repeats!
  • Take Your Time – It’s not a race, sip some tea and think it through
  • Use Hints – Stuck? Tap for a little help — we’ve got your back
  • Enjoy the Win – Sit back and admire your clever work
